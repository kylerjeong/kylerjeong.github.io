01. About_

Hi, I'm Kyler — a mechanical engineer who loves taking ideas from napkin sketch to CAD to a physical part you can hold. I care about designs that are manufacturable, tolerant of the real world, and a little bit beautiful.

My happy place is the loop between the workshop and the screen: model it, print or machine it, break it, measure why, and revise. When I'm not iterating on a prototype, I'm running simulations, tuning a mechanism, or learning a new fabrication process.
