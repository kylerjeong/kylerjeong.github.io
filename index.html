A smart lock box that keeps your phone out of reach while you work. I led a 6-person team building it around an ESP32, an OLED display, and a mechanical lock programmed in Python and C++. I designed the enclosure in Onshape and made it with laser cutting and 3D printing, revising the design on customer feedback.

  • Mechatronics

3D Modeled Wind Turbine

A wind turbine designed in SolidWorks and 3D printed for a design competition. I tuned the blade twist and angle of attack and ran FEA on the tower to get the most power without giving up structural integrity. The design took the top stiffness-to-weight ratio out of over 150 students.

  • 3D CAD
  • FEA
  • Additive Manufacturing

A multi-sensor security system that watches over package deliveries. I worked on the sensor fusion, circuit design, and 3D CAD modeling that tied everything into one autonomous detection platform.
